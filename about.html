Hello! My name is Elias and Methusela is the name I write rock music under. The style is very derivative of
my favorite bands, but I am hoping no one will notice.

The first thing about me is that I am a follower of my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. In college, God opened my eyes
to the truth of my utter depravity before a holy Judge, and that my only hope of salvation was through repenting of my love of sin and trusting in Christ. I tend to write about the most important things in my life, so naturally,
you'll find most of my music will be about God, what he has done for me, or about his Word.

I am also the husband of Yuris, the way more talented founder of Salt Stains, a Christian paper goods store.
Yuris is the greatest thing that ever happened to me apart from salvation, and a lot of my music is inspired by her.

Thanks for stopping by!

Elias
